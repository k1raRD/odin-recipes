Juicy Roasted Chicken

Description

My grandmother's recipe for roasted chicken. We are German and she used to do it this way all the time. I never have had a chicken this juicy before; this little trick works and makes the people eating it go silent. It's funny. We nibble on the celery after.

Ingredients

  • 1(3 pound) whole chicken,giblets removed
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder,or to taste
    • 4
  • Â½cup margarine,divided
  • 1 stalk celery,leaves removed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Place chicken in a roasting pan, and season generously inside and out with salt and pepper. Spinkle inside and oout with onion powder. Place 3 tablespoons margarine in the chicken cavity. Arrange dollops of the remaining margarine around the chicken's exterior. Cut celery into 3 or 4 pieces,and place in the chicken cavity

  3. Bake uncovered 1 hour and 15 minutes in the preheated oven, to a minimun internal temperature of 180 degrees F (82 degrees C). Remove from heat,and baste with melted margarine and drippings. Cover with aluminum foil, and allow to rest about 30 minutes before serving
